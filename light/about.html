ARCHITECTURE IS WHERE WE BELONG

HRO MARBNITE was founded in 2007 by Mr. Pradeep Wadhwani, a seasoned entrepreneur with a passion for natural stones. With over 16 years of experience in the marble industry in Vietnam, Mr. Wadhwani We are set out to create a company that could deliver high-quality natural stones to clients all over the world.

Our company is based in Vietnam, a country renowned for its rich deposits of natural stones. We have established long-standing relationships with the best quarries in the region, giving us access to a wide range of natural stones, including marble, granite, limestone, and much more. We are proud to deal in a variety of countries including Italy, Europe, USA, China, Pakistan, and middle eastern countries. We have supplied our products to several countries such as Turkmenistan, India, Italy, China, Pakistan, Oman, Dubai, Thailand, and Indonesia. We offer a comprehensive range of products in the form of blocks, slabs, tiles, cut-to-size, and as per client requirements. At HRO MARBNITE, we pride ourselves on our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to ensuring that our clients receive the best possible service. We take a handson approach to sourcing and processing our natural stones, ensuring that our products meet the highest standards of quality.

We have a comprehensive collection of natural stones available, including blocks, slabs, tiles, and cut-to-size products. Our products are suitable for a wide range of applications, from commercial and residential construction to landscaping and interior design.

We believe in sustainability and ethical business practices. We work with suppliers who share our values, and we ensure that our products are sourced responsibly. Our team is also committed to reducing waste and recycling whenever possible.

At HRO MARBNITE, we strive to build long-term relationships with our clients. We understand that every project is unique, and we work closely with our clients to provide tailored solutions to meet their specific needs. We take pride in delivering quality products and excellent customer service, and we believe that our success is built on the satisfaction of our clients

Marbles

Our marbles are sourced from some of the best quarries around the world and are available in a wide range of colors and patterns. From classic white Carrara marble to exotic black Marquina marble, our collection of marble products is sure to meet your aesthetic preferences. We offer marble in various finishes, including polished, honed, and brushed, allowing you to choose the perfect finish for your specific project.

01

Granites

Granite is a durable and versatile natural stone that is suitable for a wide range of applications. Our collection of granite products includes a variety of colors and patterns that can add an elegant touch to any space. From vibrant reds to subtle greys, we offer granite products in different finishes, including polished, honed, and leathered.

02

Basalts

Basalt is a unique natural stone that offers a modern and contemporary look. Our collection of basalt products is sourced from the best quarries and is available in a variety of finishes. We offer basalt products in different shades of black, grey, and brown, making it easy for you to choose the perfect option for your project.

Limestones

Limestone is a natural stone that is known for its warm and earthy tones. Our collection of limestone products is available in different colors and finishes, including honed and polished. Whether you are looking for a lightcolored limestone or a more dramatic, dark option, we have the perfect product to meet your needs.
