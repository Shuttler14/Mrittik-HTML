01

Marbles

Our marbles are sourced from some of the best quarries around the world and are available in a wide range of colors and patterns. From classic white Carrara marble to exotic black Marquina marble, our collection of marble products is sure to meet your aesthetic preferences. We offer marble in various finishes, including polished, honed, and brushed, allowing you to choose the perfect finish for your specific project.

01

Granites

Granite is a durable and versatile natural stone that is suitable for a wide range of applications. Our collection of granite products includes a variety of colors and patterns that can add an elegant touch to any space. From vibrant reds to subtle greys, we offer granite products in different finishes, including polished, honed, and leathered.

02

Basalts

Basalt is a unique natural stone that offers a modern and contemporary look. Our collection of basalt products is sourced from the best quarries and is available in a variety of finishes. We offer basalt products in different shades of black, grey, and brown, making it easy for you to choose the perfect option for your project.

03

Limestones

Limestone is a natural stone that is known for its warm and earthy tones. Our collection of limestone products is available in different colors and finishes, including honed and polished. Whether you are looking for a lightcolored limestone or a more dramatic, dark option, we have the perfect product to meet your needs.

ABOUT HRO NATURAL STONES

Welcome to HRO Natural Stones in Vietnam! We are a team of dedicated professionals who are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality marble products at competitive prices. We work with the best suppliers in the industry to ensure that our customers receive only the finest quality marble products.

Our mission is to provide our customers with exceptional service and products that will enhance the beauty and value of their homes or businesses. We take pride in our attention to detail and strive to exceed our customers' expectations every step of the way.
